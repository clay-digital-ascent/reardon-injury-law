What to Do After a Pedestrian Accident

1

Call 911

Your safety comes first. Call 911 immediately to get emergency medical responders and police to the scene. A police report is critical evidence.

2

Get Witness Names and Photos

If you're able, get contact information from anyone who saw the accident. Take photos of the scene, the vehicle, and your injuries.

3

Seek Emergency Care

Even if you feel okay at the scene, get a full medical evaluation. Adrenaline masks pain, and internal injuries may not be immediately apparent.

4

Call Reardon Injury Law Before Speaking to Any Insurer

Insurance adjusters will contact you quickly. Do not give any statements before speaking with an attorney. We protect your rights from day one.
