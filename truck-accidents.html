What to Do After a Truck Accident

1

Get Emergency Care

Truck accident injuries are often severe. Call 911 and get emergency medical treatment immediately. Your health is the top priority.

2

Preserve Evidence

Black box data and electronic logs can disappear fast. Take photos of the scene, the truck, and any visible company markings or DOT numbers.

3

Identify All Liable Parties

Truck accidents may involve the driver, trucking company, cargo loader, and maintenance provider. Multiple parties may owe you compensation.

4

Call Reardon Injury Law

Time is critical in truck accident cases. Trucking companies send investigators to the scene immediately. You need an attorney fighting for you just as fast.
