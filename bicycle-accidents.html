Bicycle Accident Injuries & Cases We Handle

Bicycle accidents often result in severe injuries because cyclists have minimal protection. We help victims of:

Car-on-Bicycle Collisions

The most common and most dangerous type of cycling accident. We hold negligent drivers fully accountable for failing to share the road safely.

Dooring Accidents

When a parked driver opens their door into a cyclist's path, the results can be catastrophic. We pursue claims against negligent drivers and their insurance.

Unsafe Road Conditions

Potholes, debris, missing signage, and poorly designed bike lanes can cause serious accidents. We identify liable government entities and pursue claims.

Hit and Run & Liability Disputes

Cyclists are often victims of hit-and-run drivers. We work with law enforcement and use all available evidence to identify responsible parties and resolve liability disputes.

What to Do After a Bicycle Accident

1

Seek Medical Care Even If You Feel Okay

Adrenaline can mask serious injuries after a cycling accident. Get a full medical evaluation immediately â€” this also creates critical documentation for your case.

2

Preserve Your Bicycle as Evidence

Do not repair or dispose of your damaged bicycle. The damage to your bike is important evidence that helps prove the severity of the impact.

3

Document the Scene and Get Witnesses

Take photos of the accident scene, the vehicle involved, road conditions, and your injuries. Get contact information from any witnesses.
