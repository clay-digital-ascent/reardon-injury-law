Motorcycle Accident Injuries & Cases We Handle

Motorcycle accidents often result in catastrophic injuries due to the lack of protection. We help victims of:

Road Rash

Severe road rash can require skin grafts and leave permanent scarring. We fight for full compensation for these painful injuries.

Traumatic Brain Injuries

Even with a helmet, TBIs are common in motorcycle crashes. These injuries can affect memory, cognition, and quality of life permanently.

Broken Bones

Fractures to arms, legs, pelvis, and ribs are extremely common and may require multiple surgeries and extensive rehabilitation.

Wrongful Death & Insurance Bad Faith

When a motorcycle accident is fatal or an insurer acts in bad faith, we hold all responsible parties accountable.

What to Do After a Motorcycle Accident

1

Seek Medical Care

Get medical attention immediately, even if injuries seem minor. Adrenaline can mask serious injuries. Medical records are critical evidence.

2

Document the Scene

Take photos of the accident scene, your motorcycle, your injuries, and any road hazards. Get witness contact information.

3

Do Not Give a Statement to the Insurer

Insurance adjusters will try to get you to say something that hurts your claim. Do not give a recorded statement without legal counsel.

4

Call Reardon Injury Law

Time is critical. Evidence disappears and witnesses forget. Contact us immediately for a free consultation about your motorcycle accident case.
