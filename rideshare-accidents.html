Rideshare Accident Injuries & Cases We Handle

Uber and Lyft accidents create unique legal challenges. We help victims in all rideshare scenarios:

Uber/Lyft Passenger Injuries

As a passenger, you're entitled to coverage under the rideshare company's $1M liability policy. We ensure you get the full compensation you deserve.

Driver-as-Victim Claims

If you're a rideshare driver injured by another motorist, navigating your own coverage plus the at-fault driver's insurance requires experienced legal guidance.

Pedestrian Strikes by Rideshare Vehicles

Distracted rideshare drivers checking their apps pose a serious risk to pedestrians. We hold them and their companies accountable.

App-On vs. App-Off Coverage Disputes

Insurance coverage changes dramatically based on whether the driver's app was on, waiting for a ride, or actively transporting. We know how to prove which period applies.

What to Do After a Rideshare Accident

1

Screenshot the Trip in the App

Before closing anything, take screenshots of your active trip, the driver's information, and the route. This evidence is critical for proving rideshare involvement.

2

Seek Medical Care

Get medical attention immediately. Many injuries don't show symptoms right away. Medical records from the day of the accident are crucial evidence.

3

Report to Uber/Lyft AND Get a Lawyer

Report the accident through the app, but do NOT give detailed statements to their insurance without legal counsel. Their adjusters work to minimize payouts.

4

Call Reardon Injury Law
