Rideshare Accident Injuries & Cases We Handle

Uber and Lyft accidents create unique legal challenges. We help victims in all rideshare scenarios:

Whiplash & Soft Tissue

Rear-end collisions are common in rideshare scenarios. Whiplash and soft tissue injuries can cause chronic pain that lasts months or years without proper treatment.

Head & Brain Injuries

Rideshare passengers often aren't braced for impact. Concussions and traumatic brain injuries can affect memory, concentration, and quality of life â€” sometimes permanently.

Back & Spinal Injuries

Herniated discs, vertebral fractures, and spinal cord damage can result from the sudden impact forces in rideshare collisions, potentially causing permanent disability.

Broken Bones

Fractured wrists, ribs, legs, and collarbones are common when rideshare vehicles are struck. These injuries often require surgery and extensive rehabilitation.

Internal Injuries

Seatbelt injuries, internal bleeding, and organ damage may not show symptoms immediately but can be life-threatening. Our medical background helps identify these hidden injuries.

Psychological Trauma & PTSD

Rideshare accidents can cause lasting anxiety, PTSD, and fear of riding in vehicles. These psychological injuries are real and compensable under California law.

What to Do After a Rideshare Accident

1

Screenshot the Trip in the App

Before closing anything, take screenshots of your active trip, the driver's information, and the route. This evidence is critical for proving rideshare involvement.

2

Seek Medical Care

Get medical attention immediately. Many injuries don't show symptoms right away. Medical records from the day of the accident are crucial evidence.

3

Report to Uber/Lyft AND Get a Lawyer

Report the accident through the app, but do NOT give detailed statements to their insurance without legal counsel. Their adjusters work to minimize payouts.

4

Call Reardon Injury Law
