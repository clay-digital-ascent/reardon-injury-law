What to Do After a Car Accident

1

Seek Medical Attention Immediately

Even if you feel fine, get checked out. Many injuries don't show symptoms until days later. This also creates crucial documentation for your case.

2

Document Everything

Take photos of the accident scene, your injuries, and all vehicle damage. Get contact information from witnesses. Keep all medical records and receipts.

3

Don't Talk to the Insurance Company

Insurance adjusters are trained to minimize your claim. Don't give a recorded statement or accept a quick settlement offer without talking to an attorney first.

4

Call Reardon Injury Law

Time is critical. Evidence disappears, witnesses forget, and statutes of limitations apply. Contact us immediately for a free consultation about your case.
